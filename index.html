A Happy Thought

The world can be a terrible place, but ...

This otter is not generated by AI. This otter is a real otter doing real otter things in the real world. How cool is that? You and I could have been born as otters. There would be a lot of swimming, playing and searching out things to eat (mostly fish — if you're not into seafood, maybe this isn't such a happy thought).

Anyway, we could have been otters. Have you ever watched an otter slide down a river bank? Or watched an otter whack an abalone shell with a rock to get at the meat inside? Otters don't worry that butter is $10 a pound and that we are hurtling into an uncertain future and no one knows where the brakes are. Think otterish thoughts: Where is the river bank? Is that pickerel tasty? Is it time to sleep on my back in a raft of otter buds holding hands and bobbing gently in the waves? YES!

Let's all be more like otters. This concludes my TED Talk.
